| T-01-10 | I | hierarchy read payload | mitigate | Return DTO projections only and avoid exposing internal metadata not needed by clients. |
| T-01-11 | D | unbounded list requests | mitigate | Enforce max limits and validated filters to prevent expensive full-table scans. |
| T-01-12 | E | endpoint access policy drift | mitigate | Centralize policy assignment and include route-role matrix tests to prevent privilege escalation. |
